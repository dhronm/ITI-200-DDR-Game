About the Game

Arrow Storm is an exciting DDR style game where players must hit targets in time with the music. The game features a endless mode where players can compete for the highest score on the leaderboard. Use the arrow keys to hit the targets as they reach the bottom of the screen. The more accurate your timing, the higher your score!
